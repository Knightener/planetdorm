About PlanetDorm

We made PlanetDorm for UMD students like you! We wanted a way for students to leave reviews for University of Maryland dorms, so that incoming and returning students would know exactly what it's like to live there before they actually move in. We plan to add many more features in the future, such as off-campus housing, a suggestion submission feature,room and floor plans, google maps reviews integration, and more enhanced descriptions on dorm types. We hope you find our website useful!

Have a suggestion or any feedback on the website?:

Search for a dorm and click on it to view its details and reviews. Read real reviews from UMD students and leave your own to contribute.

Q/A:

Q: What is the difference between a traditional dorm and a suite-style dorm?

A: Traditional dorms have shared bathrooms and common areas which are typically shared across the whole floor, while suite-style dorms have private bathrooms and living spaces shared across that suite.
